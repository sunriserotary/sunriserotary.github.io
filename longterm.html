Our Projects

A big part of our ways to give back to the community is by supporting local and international causes that we care for. Our projects include supporting non-profit organizations locally as well as working with other Rotary clubs around the world. Some of our big beneficiaries and projects are included below.

Kasese Clean Water Project

By working with the Rotary Club in Kasese, we have been fundraising towards a global grant for clean water in the Lhuhwahwa village of Kasese. The project had been going on for a few years and is finally at its last stages! With the grant we will be able to provide a sustainable clean water solution to over 1 thousand people.

Teen Kitchen Project

Teen Kitchen Project is a nonprofit organization that brings young people into the kitchen to learn to cook delicious and nourishing food. The meals they prepare are delivered to individuals and families who are in crisis due to a life-threatening illness like cancer.
