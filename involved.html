Getting Involved With Rotary

Be a Part of the Change

Chartered in 1985, we are a Club of approximately 100 high-energy men & women community and business leader volunteers. We invite you to glance through our website and our weekly bulletin, The Reveille, which will provide an insight into our weekly events and ongoing projects. Just a couple of our recent hands on projects have included collecting and packing food for the Second Harvest Food Bank, improving a school site in Chacala, Mexico, and a Hurricane Katrina relief trip to Grand Isle, Louisiana. Our fund-raisers serve not only as a means to raise money for many very worthy projects but also as social events where Rotarians and friends of Rotary can get involved. We have a full slate of annual charity events.

Rotary Members

Seeking to make a difference while having a great time? Contribute to the growth of the Santa Cruz SunriseRotary Club by becoming a member today!

Meetings and Events

Sunrise Rotary holds weekly meetings every Tuesday at 7:15AM at the Back Nine Grill & Bar located at 555 Highway 17, Santa Cruz, CA, 95060. To check out our latest and upcoming Rotary events such as fundraisers, you can view them here.
